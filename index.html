Welcome

Welcome to La Bonita Cocina. Here, we deal with all things food. We strive to provide an experience you will never forget. Weather it's a Wedding for 300, or a Wine Paring for 2, we handle all events having to do with food.

La Cocina Bonita is prepared to provide am unforgettable experience for you and your guests in one of our three dining halls, or cater to you at your location of choice. We have equipment if you would like us to serve up your event for you, or if you prefer we can utilize disposable containers at your discretion

Pedro Ariel Ardiles - Proprietor

What's Fresh

Our diverse staff come from a variety of backgrounds and professions. Our recent events include a nine course wine paring at a local Italian import store, deLuca's Fine Wines. Additionally, we recently threw a seafood themed Lobster bash for a community Golf Club. We have in the past hosted many events for the members, along with entertainment and provided alcohol service. Weddings are also very popular, and we have numerous packages to choose from.
